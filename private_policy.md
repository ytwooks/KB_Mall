**Privacy Policy of KB Mall**

1. Introduction

KB Mall ("we," "us," "our") operates the KB Mall vehicle trading platform ("Services"), including our mobile applications and website. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use and share it, your rights, and how we safeguard your information when you use our Services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information to provide our services:

Account Information:
Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, profile photo, address information

Vehicle Information:
Make, model, year, mileage, condition, vehicle photos, price, and other vehicle details

Transaction Information:
Order information, payment information, visit booking information

Communication Information:
Chat messages, voice messages, image attachments

Marketing Consent:
Marketing information receipt consent and consent date

Device Information:
Device ID, device type, operating system information, app version

Location Information:
GPS coordinates, current location, address information, region information

Usage Information:
App usage logs, search history, page view history, error logs

Session Information:
Login session, session token, refresh token

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected personal information for the following purposes:

Service Provision:
Account management, vehicle trading intermediation, real-time chat, location-based services, order management, visit booking

Service Improvement:
App performance optimization, user experience improvement, service stability enhancement

Security and Fraud Prevention:
Identity verification, fraud prevention, KYC (Know Your Customer)

Legal Compliance:
Compliance with legal requirements, dispute resolution, tax reporting

Marketing and Promotions:
Marketing information delivery, personalized advertising, service announcements

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share personal information in the following cases:

Sharing with Other Users:
Vehicle listing information, chat messages, profile information

Third-Party Service Providers:
Firebase (Google), Google Services, Apple

Legal Requirements:
Law enforcement agencies, government agencies when requested

Business Transfers:
Company acquisition, merger, asset transfer

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information as follows:

Account Information:
Retained while the account is active, retained for the period necessary to fulfill legal obligations after account deletion (generally 5 years)

Transaction Information:
Retained according to tax and commercial laws after transaction completion (generally 5 years), retained until dispute resolution is complete if a dispute occurs

Chat Messages:
Retained while the chat room is active, automatically deleted 30 days after chat room deletion

Images and Files:
Retained during service use, immediately deleted upon deletion request (backup copies are completely deleted after 30 days)

Log Information:
Error logs are automatically deleted after 90 days of retention, usage logs are automatically deleted after 1 year of retention

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights:

Access:
You can view your personal information in the app under “Settings > Profile”

Correction:
You can modify your personal information in the app under “Settings > Edit Profile”

Deletion:
You can request account deletion (information necessary to fulfill legal obligations will be retained)

Marketing Opt-Out:
You can change this in the app under “Settings > Notification Settings”

Location Services:
You can disable location services in device settings

Data Portability:
You have the right to transfer your personal information to other services

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use the following technologies:

Local Storage:
App settings storage through SharedPreferences

Cache:
Image and data caching

Session Tokens:
Login session maintenance

Firebase Analytics:
App usage statistics collection (anonymized data)

Google Analytics:
Website visit statistics (web version)

You can limit tracking in device settings.

8. Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect personal information:

Technical Security Measures:
Data encryption during transmission (HTTPS/TLS), stored data encryption, data access control through Firebase Security Rules, regular security checks

Administrative Security Measures:
Minimize personal information access permissions, employee training, audit logs

Physical Security Measures:
Utilize Firebase's security infrastructure, backup data encryption

Note: No security system is perfect, and we cannot guarantee 100% security. You are responsible for keeping your account information secure.

9. Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If a child under 18 provides personal information, we will delete such information immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Data is stored in the following locations:

Firebase Servers:
Google Cloud Platform (mainly Asia Pacific region)

Google Servers:
Google's global data centers

Data transfers are protected through standard contractual clauses and appropriate security measures.

Cambodian Personal Data Protection Law (if applicable)
